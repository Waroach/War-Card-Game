Rules of battle.

1) small scurmashes are one card each.

Draw one card and compare it against your opponent. Highest card wins, and takes both cards. (Ignore suites) Once a player has the whole deck of cards, they are declared the winner.

2) Battles of war happen when a scurmash ties.

(both players play the same amount of ) Three cards will be drawn from each deck (if available) and the fourth card from each will be compared. Highest card wins. Winner takes all 10 cards.
